## A Practical Rule

Start with shared category, then add weight for shared tags, then bias slightly toward older published posts. This keeps related links relevant without maintaining a manual cross-link map.

## Why It Helps

Readers get more context, crawlers discover adjacent pages faster, and you avoid a fragile hand-built sidebar workflow.

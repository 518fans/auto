## Why Structure Matters

A static blog scales much better when every post follows the same contract. Title, date, slug, category, tags, description, and target repository are enough to let automation handle routing, indexes, and metadata without extra manual work.

## Search Benefits

Consistent archives create stronger hubs for long-tail keywords. Category pages help crawlers understand topical clusters, while article pages stay focused on specific intent.

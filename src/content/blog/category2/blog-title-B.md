## Introduction

A lot of advice about Instagram growth still sounds as if success comes from discovering the right trick. In reality, most sustainable growth begins somewhere less exciting: with the simple question of whether people have a reason to come back.

That is also the thread running underneath [this discussion of real growth on Instagram](https://collaboratory.gse.upenn.edu/sites/default/files/webform/understanding-real-growth-on-instagram.pdf).

## Reach Can Introduce You, but Return Builds You

Many accounts are built around discovery tactics alone. They optimize every post for exposure, but very little for retention.

## Why Return Matters More Than a Single Win

Habits form when the account repeatedly delivers a familiar kind of value in a voice people trust.

## Conclusion

Real growth usually favors continuity over novelty. Give people a reason to recognize you, then give them a reason to keep caring.
